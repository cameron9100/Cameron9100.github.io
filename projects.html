Oklahoma City Service Organizations

  • Thunder Cares© Organization

    The Thunder Cares© Organization works to create programs that make a positive impact on the greater Oklahoma City community. Their primary focus is on youth, with an emphasis on reading and literacy, health and fitness, and, of course, basketball.

    To see the Thunder Cares© Organization in action, click here!

  • Around the Holidays

    During the course of the holiday season, the Oklahoma City Thunder organization participates in the annual "Thunder Blue Christmas", where the players help fulfill childrens' wishlists in supporting Target stores.
