Mascot

Rumble the Bison is the face of the Oklahoma City Thunder. Introduced on February 17, 2009, Rumble's name comes from the sound thunder makes. On August 13, 2009, Rumble was awarded the NBA's Mascot of the Year Award due to his tremendous interactions with fans. You can see him in action giving people high-fives, hugs, and team oriented t-shirts.
